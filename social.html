Social

Explore our social side

Doing well in school doesn't mean we don't have fun

We participate in a wide variety of events including social events with other organizations, brotherhood events, philanthropy events, intramural sports and more.

Our Social Events

Formals

Every semester, the brothers head down to Washington, D.C. for a night of dancing, great company, and lasting memories.

Away Week

We organize fraternity wide "away week" trips that range from the mountains of Colorado to the beaches of the Outer Banks.

Philanthropy

We volunteer with a number of organizations including the KEEN Institute in DC and the FIRST National Robotics League.

Intramurals

In addition to our stellar soccer team, we also participate in intramural basketball, volleyball, flag football, and more.

Tailgates

Our tailgates are all about bringing people together to celebrate game day with food, music, games, and school spirit.

Big Little

Big Little is one of our most cherished traditions, pairing older members (Bigs) with new members (Littles) to create lasting bonds.

So yeah, we know how to balance our time.
